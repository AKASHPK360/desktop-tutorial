Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account, follow these steps within the app:

  1. Open the Nexoraa app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion. Your username, email, and all associated personal data will be permanently removed.

Delete Some or All Data Without Deleting Account

You can request deletion of specific data without removing your account. This includes usage data, preferences, or photos. Send a request to our support email:

Request Data Deletion

Data deletion requests will be processed within 90 days.
