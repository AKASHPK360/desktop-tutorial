Nexoraa - Your Privacy Matters

Overview

At Nexoraa, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your data from our systems.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you submit a deletion request, we will remove the following information:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile details)
  • User-generated content and activity history
  • Authentication tokens and session data
  • Any other personally identifiable information we store
Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance, security purposes, or to fulfill contractual obligations. This includes transaction records, audit logs, and data required by law.

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

In some cases, processing may take longer due to:

  • Legal or regulatory requirements

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion requests, we may ask you to verify your identity by providing:

  • Additional account information
  • Response to security questions
  • Confirmation from your registered email address
