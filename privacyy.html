Nexoraa – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nexoraa ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

2. Information We Collect

Nexoraa may collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Email, password, authentication info (via Firebase Auth).
  • Usage Data: App activity, interactions, basic analytics.
  • Location Data: Only when required and granted by the user.
  • Media: Photos or files you choose to upload.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide account login and authentication.
  • To store user-generated content securely.
  • To improve app performance, features, and stability.
  • To deliver relevant ads (Google AdMob).

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services, and all data transmitted is encrypted in transit.

5. Third-Party Services

Nexoraa uses:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Database, Storage)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Ads + AD_ID)
  • Google Maps / Location Services
  • OpenAI APIs for AI-based features

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not designed for children under 13.

Account & Data Deletion Request

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

1. How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

akashpk360@gmail.com

Use the subject line: "Nexoraa Account Deletion Request"

2. Information You Must Include

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (optional)
  • Date of request

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your Firebase account
  • Your stored files/photos
  • Your app preferences
  • Any data stored in the database

4. Data We May Retain

Some logs may be temporarily retained for security or legal reasons (max 90 days).

5. Data Deletion Timeline

Account deletion is typically completed within 7 days.
